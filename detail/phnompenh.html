Tuol Sleng Genocide Museum (S-21)

A former high school turned into a Khmer Rouge torture and interrogation center. Walk through the haunting rooms, see survivor portraits, and learn about the 17,000+ prisoners who passed through. A somber but essential stop to understand Cambodia's recent history.
