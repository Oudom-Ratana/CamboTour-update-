Explore 25

provinces in Cambodia

Battambang

Ready for a dash of adrenaline? Hop on the world-famous Bamboo Train, explore the ancient caves turned into shrines of peace, and witness the breathtaking 'Bat Exodus' at twilight.

Siem Reap

The gateway to the Angkor Empire. Walk through the majestic stone faces of Bayon, watch the sunrise over Angkor Wat, and enjoy the vibrant nightlife of Pub Street.

Phnom Penh

Cambodia’s bustling capital where tradition meets the modern skyline. Visit the shimmering Royal Palace, stroll the riverside promenade, and dive into history at the National Museum.

Kampot

Relax by the Preaek Tuek Chhu river. Famous for its world-class pepper, colonial architecture, and the misty Bokor Mountain, Kampot is the ultimate spot for slow-travel lovers.

Sihanoukville

Your bridge to paradise. From here, set sail for the white sands of Koh Rong and Koh Rong Samloem to swim with bioluminescent plankton under a starry sky.

Mondulkiri

Escape to the rolling "Sea of Forests." Trek through lush jungles, bathe with elephants in ethical sanctuaries, and feel the mist of the majestic Bousra Waterfall.

Kep

Taste the freshest seafood at the iconic Crab Market.This quiet seaside retreat offers sunset views over the Gulf of Thailand, lush hiking trails in Kep National Park, and a touch of colonial ruins.

Kratie

A peaceful town on the banks of the Mekong. It’s the best place in the world to spot the rare Irrawaddy dolphins and enjoy a bicycle ride around the idyllic Koh Trong island.

Ratanakiri

The wild "Red Province" of the Northeast. Swim in thecrystal-clear waters of the Yeak Laom volcanic lake, discover hidden waterfalls, and explore the indigenous cultures of the highlands.

Preah Vihear

Home to a breathtaking temple perched on a cliff edge. Experience the power of the Angkorian era with panoramic views of the plains below at this stunning UNESCO World Heritage site.

Koh Kong

The wild frontier. Kayak through Southeast Asia's largest mangrove forest, explore the Cardamom Mountains, and discover secret beaches on the largest island in Cambodia.

Pursat

Famous for its exquisite marble carvings and the unique floating village of Kampong Luong. Explore the vastness of the Tonle Sap lake and the scenic mountain roads of the West.

Takeo

Known as the "Cradle of Khmer Civilization." Explore the ancient hilltop temples of Phnom Da and Phnom Chisor, or take a boat through the vast canals of Angkor Borei.

Kampong Cham

Experience the charm of a riverside town famous for its seasonal bamboo bridge. Visit the Wat Nokor Bachey ruins and enjoy the peaceful island life of Koh Paen.

Kandal

The spiritual heart surrounding the capital. Climb the sacred hills of Oudong to see royal stupas, visit Island" (Koh Dach) to watch traditional weavers at work, and explore the ancient pagodas along the Mekong riverbanks.

Kampong Thom

Home to Sambor Prei Kuk, a forest-enveloped UNESCO site older than Angkor Wat. It’s a quiet gateway to the countryside, perfect for seeing authentic village life.

Banteay Meanchey

Discover the "Great Citadel" of Banteay Chhmar. This remote temple site offers a rare, peaceful exploration of massive stone carvings without the crowds of Siem Reap.

Prey Veng

The agricultural heart of Cambodia. Witness endless emerald rice fields, explore the Neak Loeung Bridge views, and see the traditional life of the Mekong plains.

Kampong Speu

Adventure awaits at Kirirom National Park. Famous for its pine forests, cool mountain air, and waterfalls, it’s the perfect nature getaway just outside the capital.

Kampong Chhnang

The "Port of Pottery." Famous for its traditional clay heritage and the sprawling floating villages of the Tonle Sap. A photographer's dream for capturing authentic river life.

Steung Treng

Where the Sekong and Mekong rivers meet. An eco-tourism hub perfect for kayaking through flooded forests, bird watching, and crossing the border into the wild north.

Svay Rieng

The bustling gateway to Vietnam. Visit the lively border town of Bavet, enjoy the scenic Waiko River, and witness the rapid industrial growth of the Southeast.

Pailin

Nestled in the foothills of the Cardamom Mountains. Once famous for precious gems, it is now a peaceful retreat known for the stunning Wat Phnom Yat and fresh mountain air.

Oddar Meanchey

A land of rugged history and natural beauty. Explore the Dangrek Mountains, historic sites, and the quiet resilience of Cambodia's northernmost frontier.

Tboung Khmum

The kingdom's newest province and agricultural powerhouse. Wander through vast rubber plantations and experience the vibrant local markets of the Mekong delta.
