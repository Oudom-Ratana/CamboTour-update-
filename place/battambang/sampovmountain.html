Natural Wonders

The Famous Bat Eruption

Phnom Sampeau Mountain

Battambang Province, Banan District

📍 12 km southwest of Battambang city
☀️ Nov — Mar (Cool Season)

History

Phnom Sampeau ("The Ship Mountain") is a 100-meter-high limestone hill with deep historical and spiritual significance. The summit features a golden stupa, several Buddhist shrines, and the notorious "Killing Caves" – a tragic reminder of the Khmer Rouge regime where victims were thrown into a deep hole.

Every evening around sunset, millions of wrinkle-lipped bats emerge from a cave on the mountainside, creating a spectacular natural phenomenon that draws visitors from around the world. The mountain also offers panoramic views of the surrounding rice paddies and Battambang countryside.

Visitor Guide

✓ Things You Can Do

  • • Watch the sunset and bat eruption from the western viewpoint
  • • Hike or take a moto to the summit
  • • Visit the Killing Caves memorial (respectful silence)
  • • Explore the golden stupa and Buddha statues

✗ Things You Can't

  • • Make loud noise inside the Killing Caves memorial
  • • Touch or remove any bones/artifacts
  • • Use flash photography inside the sacred caves
  • • Climb on the stupa or statues
